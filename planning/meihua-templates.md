## 基础概念

| 数字 | 卦名 | 英文 | 核心含义 | 能量特质 |

| 1 | 乾 | Heaven | 创造、领导、开创 | 积极、主动 |
| 2 | 兑 | Lake | 喜悦、沟通、开放 | 流动、表达 |
| 3 | 离 | Fire | 光明、洞察、热情 | 清晰、转化 |

### 六十四卦简化解读框架

每个卦象由上下两卦组成，结合动爻变化，形成具体建议。

---

## 卦象解读模板

### 解读结构

```
1. 卦象名称（中英文）
2. 核心主题（一句话）
3. 当前能量状态描述
4. 行动建议（3条具体）
5. 时机判断（Now / Wait / Adjust）
6. 风险提示（1条）
```

---

## 常见卦象解读示例

### 1. 乾为天 (Heaven over Heaven)
**核心主题：** 开创与领导力的峰值时刻

**能量状态：**
Your energy is in a powerful creative phase. This is a time for bold action, leadership, and initiating what you've been planning.

**行动建议：**
1. **Take the Lead** — Step forward with confidence. Your vision is clear and the timing supports action.
2. **Start What Matters** — Begin that project, have that conversation, make that decision you've been postponing.
3. **Maintain Integrity** — Success comes from alignment with your core values, not just ambition.

**时机判断：** NOW — This is your window for decisive action.

**风险提示：** Avoid arrogance. Power without humility creates resistance.

---

### 2. 坤为地 (Earth over Earth)
**核心主题：** 接纳与孕育的智慧

**能量状态：**
You are in a receptive, nurturing phase. This is not a time for forcing outcomes, but for allowing things to unfold naturally.

**行动建议：**
1. **Practice Receptivity** — Open yourself to support, ideas, and opportunities that come to you.
2. **Nurture What Exists** — Focus on caring for current projects and relationships rather than starting new ones.
3. **Trust the Process** — Results may not be visible yet, but groundwork is being laid.

**时机判断：** WAIT — Allow things to develop organically.

**风险提示：** Don't mistake passivity for receptivity. Stay engaged while allowing.

---

### 3. 水雷屯 (Water over Thunder) — 起始之难
**核心主题：** 新开始的挑战与机遇

**能量状态：**
You are at the beginning of something new. Energy is building but not yet flowing smoothly. Expect some resistance.

**行动建议：**
1. **Start Small** — Don't try to do everything at once. Focus on the first step.
2. **Build Foundations** — Invest time in preparation and planning now.
3. **Persist Through Difficulty** — Initial challenges are normal; they test your commitment.

**时机判断：** ADJUST — Proceed with caution and preparation.

**风险提示：** Don't rush. Premature action wastes energy.

---

### 4. 山水蒙 (Mountain over Water) — 启蒙与学习
**核心主题：** 学习与探索的阶段

**能量状态：**
You are in a learning phase. What you need to know will be revealed through experience, not just study.

**行动建议：**
1. **Stay Curious** — Approach the situation as a student, even if you're experienced.
2. **Seek Guidance** — A mentor or teacher can accelerate your learning.
3. **Learn by Doing** — Theory isn't enough; take small actions to test and learn.

**时机判断：** WAIT — Gather information before major decisions.

**风险提示：** Don't pretend to know what you don't. Humility accelerates growth.

---

### 5. 水天需 (Water over Heaven) — 等待与准备
**核心主题：** 耐心的智慧

**能量状态：**
The timing isn't quite right yet. Your role now is to prepare and position yourself for when the moment arrives.

**行动建议：**
1. **Prepare Thoroughly** — Use this time to build skills, resources, and relationships.
2. **Stay Alert** — Watch for signs that conditions are shifting.
3. **Cultivate Patience** — Forcing action now wastes energy and creates complications.

**时机判断：** WAIT — Conditions are not yet optimal.

**风险提示：** Don't confuse waiting with inaction. Preparation is active work.

---

### 6. 天水讼 (Heaven over Water) — 冲突与分歧
**核心主题：** 处理分歧与争议

**能量状态：**
There is tension or conflict in the situation. Direct confrontation may not be the best path.

**行动建议：**
1. **Seek Middle Ground** — Look for solutions that address core needs of all parties.
2. **Choose Battles Wisely** — Not every disagreement needs to be resolved now.
3. **Focus on Interests, Not Positions** — Understand what people really need, not just what they say.

**时机判断：** ADJUST — Change approach, not necessarily timing.

**风险提示：** Winning an argument can mean losing a relationship. Choose wisely.

---

### 7. 地水师 (Earth over Water) — 团队与组织
**核心主题：** 集体行动与领导力

**能量状态：**
This situation requires coordinated effort. Your role may be to lead, follow, or facilitate collaboration.

**行动建议：**
1. **Clarify Roles** — Make sure everyone understands their part and how it contributes to the whole.
2. **Build Trust** — Team success depends on mutual confidence and clear communication.
3. **Lead by Example** — Your actions set the standard for others.

**时机判断：** NOW — Collective energy supports action.

**风险提示：** Without clear direction, group energy scatters.

---

### 8. 水地比 (Water over Earth) — 联盟与亲近
**核心主题：** 建立有益的关系

**能量状态：**
This is a favorable time for building alliances, partnerships, and close connections.

**行动建议：**
1. **Reach Out** — Initiate contact with people who share your values and goals.
2. **Offer Support First** — Generosity creates reciprocity.
3. **Choose Allies Carefully** — Quality of connection matters more than quantity.

**时机判断：** NOW — Relationship energy is favorable.

**风险提示：** Don't compromise your integrity to please others.

## 动爻变化解读

动爻表示变化的焦点，影响卦象的解读重点：

| 动爻位置 | 含义 | 建议重点 |
|---------|------|---------|
| 初爻（底部） | 基础/开始 | 关注起点和基础条件 |
| 二爻 | 发展/积累 | 关注过程中的调整 |
| 三爻 | 转折/挑战 | 关注关键决策点 |
| 四爻 | 转变/突破 | 关注时机和突破口 |
| 五爻 | 成熟/领导 | 关注责任和影响力 |
| 上爻（顶部） | 完成/超越 | 关注收尾和超越 |

---

## 时机判断标准

### NOW（立即行动）
- 卦象显示能量流动顺畅
- 动爻在有利位置
- 上下卦能量协调

### WAIT（等待时机）
- 卦象显示能量尚未成熟
- 需要更多准备或信息
- 外部条件不完全具备

### ADJUST（调整策略）
- 卦象显示有阻力或冲突
- 需要改变方法而非放弃
- 部分条件具备，部分需要调整

## 使用说明

1. **计算卦象** — 使用 (A+时+分)%8 得上卦，(B+时+分)%8 得下卦，(A+B+C+时+分)%6 得动爻
2. **查找解读** — 根据上下卦组合找到对应解读
3. **结合动爻** — 根据动爻位置调整建议重点
4. **现代转化** — 使用上述模板语言，避免传统术语
