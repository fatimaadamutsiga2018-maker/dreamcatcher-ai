# ClarityPath 结果文案模板

## 能量类型总结（4种）

### 1. 高能状态（High Flow）
**总分区间：** 8.5 - 10.0

**一句话总结：**
> "Your inner resources are abundant right now. This is your window to turn vision into action. Trust your intuition and take the next step with confidence."

**详细解读：**
You are operating at peak energy. Your mental clarity, physical vitality, and motivation are aligned, creating a powerful state of flow. This is the perfect time to:
- Launch new projects or initiatives
- Make important decisions
- Have difficult conversations
- Push beyond your comfort zone

**提醒：**
While this is a time for action, remember to pace yourself. High energy is a gift—use it wisely on what truly matters.

---

### 2. 平衡状态（Steady State）
**总分区间：** 6.5 - 8.4

**一句话总结：**
> "Your energy is stable and sustainable. This is a time for consolidation, refinement, and steady progress rather than dramatic leaps."

**详细解读：**
You are in a sustainable rhythm. Your energy isn't peaking, but it's consistent and reliable. This phase is ideal for:
- Refining existing projects
- Building systems and habits
- Maintaining important relationships
- Preparing for future opportunities

**提醒：**
Don't mistake steadiness for stagnation. This is where lasting progress is built, one consistent step at a time.

---

### 3. 调整期（Recalibration）
**总分区间：** 4.5 - 6.4

**一句话总结：**
> "Your energy is recalibrating. Rather than pushing forward, this is a time to pause, reflect, and realign with what truly matters to you."

**详细解读：**
You may feel some inconsistency—moments of clarity mixed with uncertainty. This is a natural phase of recalibration. Focus on:
- Slowing down and listening to yourself
- Reassessing your priorities
- Letting go of what no longer serves you
- Gathering information before making big decisions

**提醒：**
This is not a setback—it's a necessary reset. Trust the process of realignment.

---

### 4. 充电期（Recharge Mode）
**总分区间：** 1.0 - 4.4

**一句话总结：**
> "Your energy reserves are low. This is not a time for pushing—it's a time for rest, restoration, and gentle self-care."

**详细解读：**
Your mind, body, or circumstances are signaling a need for rest. Honor this by:
- Prioritizing sleep and physical restoration
- Reducing commitments and obligations
- Seeking support from others
- Engaging in activities that replenish rather than deplete you

**提醒：**
Rest is not laziness—it's wisdom. By recharging now, you build capacity for what's ahead.

## 维度建议（按最低分维度）

### 心理状态维度最低

**核心信息：**
Your mental clarity needs attention. A scattered mind makes everything harder.

**3条行动建议：**
1. **Morning Brain Dump** — Spend 5 minutes each morning writing down everything on your mind. Don't organize it, just get it out.

2. **Single-Tasking Blocks** — Choose one important task. Set a timer for 25 minutes. Focus only on that task. No phone, no notifications.

3. **Digital Sunset** — Create a boundary with technology. No screens 1 hour before bed. Let your mind actually rest.

---

### 身体状态维度最低

**核心信息：**
Your physical energy is depleted. Your body is asking for care.

**3条行动建议：**
1. **Sleep First** — Make sleep your top priority for the next 7 days. Aim for 7-8 hours. Everything else can wait.

2. **Gentle Movement** — Not intense exercise. A 20-minute walk, stretching, or yoga. Movement that energizes, not depletes.

3. **Nourishment Audit** — Notice what you're eating and drinking. Add one nutrient-dense meal today. Small steps compound.

---

### 生活满意度维度最低

**核心信息：**
There's a gap between where you are and where you want to be. This is information, not failure.

**3条行动建议：**
1. **The One Thing** — Identify the ONE area (work, relationship, or finance) that would most improve your life if it changed. Focus there.

2. **Micro-Conversation** — Have one honest conversation this week. It doesn't have to solve everything—just open the door.

3. **Gratitude + Action** — Write down 3 things you're grateful for in this area. Then write one small action you could take.

---

### 成长动力维度最低

**核心信息：**
Your growth momentum has slowed. This often happens when we've been pushing too hard for too long.

**3条行动建议：**
1. **Curiosity Spark** — What are you naturally curious about right now? Not what you "should" learn—what actually interests you? Follow that.

2. **Tiny Experiment** — Commit to just 15 minutes a day on something new. So small it feels almost too easy. Consistency beats intensity.

3. **Reconnect with "Why"** — Remind yourself why growth matters to you. Not external expectations—your own deeper reasons.
