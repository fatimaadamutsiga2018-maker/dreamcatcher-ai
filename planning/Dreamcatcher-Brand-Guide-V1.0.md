# Dreamcatcher AI — Brand & Content Guide

## Core Philosophy

**One sentence:**

> Life is not only about effort. It is about energy, timing, and alignment.

**The equation:**

```
Success = (Internal State × Environmental Timing) + Strategic Action
```

- **Internal State**: Your current cognitive and emotional battery
- **Environmental Timing**: The friction or flow of the world around you
- **Strategic Action**: Doing the *right* thing, not just *anything*

**The positive cycle:**

```
Better Choices → Elevated Energy → Easier Life → Better Choices
```

## Brand Identity

### Who We Are

**Role**: Insightful Observer — a mentor and friend

**Metaphor**: We help you read the "weather" of your life, so you can decide whether to sail or anchor.

**We are:**
- A calm, warm, insightful companion
- Grounded in science, expressed in human language
- Solutions-oriented, never fear-based

**We are NOT:**
- A fortune teller or destiny predictor
- A mystical guru or AI divination tool
- A productivity grinder pushing "hustle culture"

### Voice & Tone

| Situation | Tone |
|-----------|------|
| User is confused | Warm, clarifying, patient |
| User is stuck | Encouraging, reframing |
| User is curious | Engaging, insightful |
| Explaining science | Accessible, grounded |

**Instead of:** "The stars say it's a bad day."
**We say:** "You're running on fumes and the world isn't helping today. Skip the hard conversations — recharge first."

## The Three-Layer Language System

### Layer 1: User-Facing (what users see in the product)

Simple, clear, human. No jargon.

| Use | Don't Use |
|-----|-----------|
| timing | destiny, fate |
| energy | cosmic force, chi |
| alignment | karma, fortune |
| momentum | luck |
| friction | bad energy |
| flow | divine guidance |
| recharge | spiritual healing |
| low energy / drained | negative energy |

**Example:**
> Today favors preparation over bold action.

### Layer 2: Blog & Content (explanations for curious readers)

80% human language + 20% science. Accessible depth.

Acceptable terms:
- cognitive load
- decision fatigue
- circadian rhythm
- energy windows
- sleep pressure
- flow state

**Example:**
> Your brain has natural energy cycles during the day. Working with them — instead of against them — is the difference between flow and frustration.

### Layer 3: Internal & Theory (brand strategy docs only)

Full scientific framing. Never shown to end users.

- chronobiology
- behavioral science
- system dynamics
- bio-energetics
- suprachiasmatic nucleus

## Energy State System: 6 Types

These are **current states**, not personality types. A person can be Kinetic today and Restorative tomorrow.

| Type | Characteristics | Best Activities |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Kinetic** (行动能量) | High physical energy, wants to push forward | Start tasks, clear backlogs, execute fast |
| **Strategic** (战略能量) | Clear mind, moderate action drive | Plan, think long-term, structure problems |
| **Reflective** (反思能量) | Inward-focused, deep thinking | Review, write, learn |
| **Connector** (社交能量) | Strong social resonance | Negotiate, build relationships, communicate |
| **Adaptive** (变化能量) | Flexible, high adaptability | Handle surprises, adjust plans quickly |
| **Restorative** (恢复能量) | Low energy, needs recovery | Rest, organize systems, reduce decisions |

### How Energy Types Map to Product

- **Energy State Test** → Identifies current type
- **Daily Activity Guide** → Suggests activities matching your type + today's timing
- **Hexagram Reading** → Deeper decision support when type + timing aren't enough

## Energy State Test Design

**Format:** 12–15 questions, 4 dimensions

### Dimension 1: Energy Level
- How mentally clear do you feel today?
- How physically energized do you feel?
- How easy is it to focus today?

### Dimension 2: Emotional State
- How stable do your emotions feel today?
- How motivated do you feel to act?

### Dimension 3: Cognitive Load
- Do you feel mentally overloaded?
- Are there many unresolved tasks in your mind?

### Dimension 4: Social Energy
- Do you feel like interacting with people today?
- Would you rather work alone or collaborate?

**Output:** Maps to one of the 6 Energy Types → personalized activity recommendations.

## Writing Guidelines

### The 80/20 Rule

All user-facing content follows: **80% human language + 20% science**.

| Element | Ratio |
|---------|-------|
| Personal stories, analogies, everyday language | 80% |
| Scientific terms (cognitive load, circadian rhythm, etc.) | 20% |

### Article Structure Template

```
1. Hook — relatable scenario (2-3 sentences)
2. Problem — why the old way doesn't work
3. Reframe — introduce the Dreamcatcher perspective
4. Science — one grounded concept (keep it brief)
5. Practical — what the reader can do today
6. Bridge — subtle connection to Dreamcatcher (never hard sell)
```

### Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Write like a smart friend explaining over coffee
- Use "you" and "your" — make it personal
- Ground claims in observable experience first, science second
- End with something the reader can try today
- Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences max)

**Don't:**
- Use mystical or fortune-telling language
- Promise specific outcomes ("you WILL succeed")
- Use fear-based framing ("if you don't do this...")
- Over-explain the science (one concept per article)
- Hard-sell the product in blog content
