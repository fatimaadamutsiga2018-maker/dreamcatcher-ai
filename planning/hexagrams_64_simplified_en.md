## 5-Level Result Scale

| Level | Icon | Label | User-Friendly Description |
|-------|------|-------|---------------------------|
| 1 | 🟢 | Smooth Success | Go with the flow, everything aligns |
| 2 | 🟡 | Likely Success | Good odds, proceed with confidence |
| 3 | 🟠 | Success with Effort | Obstacles present, persistence needed |
| 4 | 🔴 | Low Probability | Timing off, consider waiting |
| 5 | ⚫ | Avoid | Against the current, not recommended |
